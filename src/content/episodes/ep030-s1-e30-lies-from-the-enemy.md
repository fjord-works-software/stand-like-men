Our culture is full of catchy sayings—“Follow your heart,” “Live your truth,” “God just wants you to be happy”—but do these ideas actually lead us toward truth, or away from it?  In this episode, we unpack some of the most common cultural lies and half-truths that sound good on the surface but fall apart under the weight of Scripture.  We talk about where real wisdom comes from, the danger of elevating feelings over truth, and how to navigate a world full of well-packaged deception with spiritual clarity and courage.
